KU faculty specific styling

In order to display a page with the colors of a certain faculty, a class must be added to the <body> tag.

For example, <body theme-science> will by default produce link colors, branding, etc. relative to the faculty of Science.

Possible classes are:

  1. <body> (no class) Default KU styling
  2. <body theme-samf> Faculty of Social Sciences
  3. <body theme-sund> Faculty of Health Sciences
  4. <body theme-science> Faculty of Science
  5. <body theme-jura> Faculty of Law
  6. <body theme-teol> Faculty of Theology
  7. <body theme-hum> Faculty of Humanities
