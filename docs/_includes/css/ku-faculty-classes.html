KU faculty specific styling

In order to display a page with the colors of a certain faculty, a class must be added to the <body> tag. Test the colors here.

For example, <body class="theme-science"> will by default produce link colors, branding, etc. relative to the faculty of Science.

Possible classes are:

  1. <body> (no class) Default KU styling
  2. <body class="theme-samf"> Faculty of Social Sciences
  3. <body class="theme-sund"> Faculty of Health Sciences
  4. <body class="theme-science"> Faculty of Science
  5. <body class="theme-jura"> Faculty of Law
  6. <body class="theme-teol"> Faculty of Theology
  7. <body class="theme-hum"> Faculty of Humanities
