KU faculty specific styling

In order to display a page with the colors of a certain faculty, a class must be added to the <html> tag. Test the colors here.

For example, <html class="theme-science"> will by default produce link colors, branding, etc. relative to the faculty of Science.

Possible classes are:

  1. <html class="theme-faelles"> Default KU styling
  2. <html class="theme-samf"> Faculty of Social Sciences
  3. <html class="theme-sund"> Faculty of Health Sciences
  4. <html class="theme-science"> Faculty of Science
  5. <html class="theme-jur"> Faculty of Law
  6. <html class="theme-teo"> Faculty of Theology
  7. <html class="theme-hum"> Faculty of Humanities
